UofT CS Faculty Change Over Time

Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto — 2007–present

Data was collected from archived snapshots of the UofT CS faculty directory via the Wayback Machine, covering roughly one snapshot per month from November 2007 to the present. “Appearance” and “departure” refer to when a person’s name was first or last visible on the department website — not necessarily their actual hiring or departure dates. Stream classification (research vs. teaching) is drawn from section headers and title strings on the modern site (2021+); earlier snapshots are inferred from title keywords or, for pre-2009 data, may be uncertain. The “≤2007” cohort represents the 69 faculty already listed when data collection began; their true start dates predate these records. Vibe coded with Claude Code. Source on GitHub.
